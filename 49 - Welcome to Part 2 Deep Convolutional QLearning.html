Welcome to Part 2!

In this Part we will build a more advanced AI model called Deep Convolutional Q-Learning. It simply consists of adding to Deep Q-Learning a Convolutional Neural Network (see the Annex at the end of this course) so that our AI can see images, which will be the inputs of the whole Neural Network.

Once we build the Neural Network, thus composed of first a Convolutional Neural Network, followed by a fully-connected Neural Network, we will train it with the Deep Q-Learning model to play Doom, this famous game where you have to reach a destination without getting killed by monsters.
